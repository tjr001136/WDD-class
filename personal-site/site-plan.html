Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to help students who have difficulty in each subject. The website will connect tutors with students in need to help them directly.

Audience

The people who visit my website will be students who fall behind in their studies or tutors who want to help those students. I think many tutors will also be interested because they will receive a certain amount of tuition from students. Students will also be interested in cheaper prices than traditional personal tutoring.

Content

Home page

[Most students are very interested in academic performance. In fact, good academic performance can provide them with a better future and a better job. Therefore, many students will make great efforts to improve their academic performance. However, while some students grow up well on their own, others cannot study on their own without someone's help. I made this website for these students. The website is designed to allow students with poor academic performance to learn many things from tutors. Tutors are also made up of students of similar ages, which will bring familiarity to students. Tutors will be guaranteed some revenue. There will be more profit than just working part-time. Also, I think you will feel satisfied by the achievements of other students. I'm sure many students will ask questions about how to find a tutor. It will identify individual requirements through the survey and then connect the tutor and the student directly. Students will be able to find the right tutor for them as soon as possible by simply entering a few short questions and simple personal information.]
